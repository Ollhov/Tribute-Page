Meryl Streep

Mama Mia, here we go again!
Born on June 22nd, 1949, Meryl Streep is an award winning and versatile actress.
She has even described as the "best actress of her generation".
She has extreme accent and character adaptability, making her fit for practically any role.
Staring off as an actress on the stage, and starring in a few small series, Meryl then established herself as a film actress in the 1980's.
Streep never let time bring her down, continuing to star in films and series even to present day!
